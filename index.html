Bride’s story

Born in Purworejo, Ruli is an extrovert who loves drawing and keens on writing. Ruli finished her school in SMA Taruna Nusantara Magelang which she continued her education in Medicine UGM. she loves travelling and had spent several times overseas on conferences and internships. in the university, she actives on both academics and non-academics which briought her to be awarded as one of the outstanding students in the faculty. Currently, Ruli is finishing her clinical years in faculty of medicine UGM

Groom’s story

Different from Ruli, Thoriq who comes from Purwokerto is an introvert. Thoriq was an alumnus of MAN Insan Cendekia Serpong and Bachelor of Engineering from National University of Singapore. He is an aspiring technopreneur and research commercialization consultant who has strong background in life science. He is also a biomedical engineer who excels in medical device development and a developer who keen to develop software and hardware. Currently, Thoriq is in his second year as a PhD-MBA double degree student in NUS Graduate School for Integrated Science and Engineering

Timeline

  • Jury Guide IBO Bali July 2014

    Bali 2014 was the first time they met and knew each other, although they are both competitor at two very same events, GAMAMED fair 2009 as well as National Biology Olympiad 2010 Medan. Having the same passion on the competition, they are back to take part in the international biology Olympiad as volunteers. Unintentionally, both of them are assigned to be volunteers for jury guide.

  • May 2015 : FIM 17

    Suggested by their own friends, Thoriq and Ruli both applied for Forum Indonesia Muda batch 17 to expand their networks with the best people from the countries. From over 5000 applicants, they are accepted in the program and destined to meet in this networking event and learn together.

  • September 2015 : ESC Berlin

    Having similar study background and research in medical field, they both submitted their own research works to ESC Berlin and were accepted in the program. Once again, they are destined to meet and travel together to quench their passions. They even worked together to find for travel grants and sponsorships for the conference.

  • December 2016 “tumbuh”

    Based on different skillsets, background but shared same vision to contribute, while too busy on their heavy workload study, they both decide to collaborate to share their interesting stories, individually and together in a book. As they are inspired by change, transformation and growing together, they named the book “tumbuh”.

    And We are here, the day we could start our new life and struggle together for the rest of our life
